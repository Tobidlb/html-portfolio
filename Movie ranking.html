The Best Korean Series According to Me

My top 3 Korean dramas of all-time

When the Phone rings

This drama is simply one of the best k-dramas I've watched so far. The cast is top tier and the acting is good too.

Tales of the Nine-tailed [Season 2]

I loved the first season but the second season has no competition. It's simply the best.

A virtuous business

It's about four women who venture into a business that doesn't align with the conservative nature of Koreans in the 1990s
